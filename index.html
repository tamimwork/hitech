Our History

Since the 1980s, the Rahman brothers have grown from the iron and steel business to the garments industry with rahman knit garments ltd., and later strengthened their legacy by establishing hi-tech steel & re-rolling mills ltd.

Our Mission

Our Vision

To provide its customers with the best possible satisfaction and respect for their investment by facilitating them with strong and resilient infrastructure solution.

To emerge as a premier manufacturer of steel that will provide the nation a strong and reliable infrastructure.

"As they always cherished the aspiration to be a bright figure in their past business, they paid great attention to re-establish the business in an entirely new and broadened way by founding Hi-Tech Steel and Re-rolling Mills Ltd. a world-class steel melting and re-rolling mill with the mission to keep pace with the ever growing demand of steel in Bangladesh."

Manufacturing

From molten metal to monumental strength

Induction Melting Furnace

Hi-Tech has an Induction Melting Furnace with a capacity of 8 Metric ton. This high capacity unit is manufactured by Megatherm Furnaces Pvt. Ltd., India. Scraps are melted into liquid steel here.

View More

Continuous casting machine

Hi-Tech has an Induction Melting Furnace with a capacity of 8 Metric ton. This high capacity unit is manufactured by Megatherm Furnaces Pvt. Ltd., India. Scraps are melted into liquid steel here. The plant operates 3 furnaces of 8 Metric Ton capacity each, allowing uninterrupted production to run continuously.

View More

Re-heating Furnace

Natural gas fired re-heating furnace equipped with motor driven mechanical pusher. Steel Bars are heated in rolling temperature (1100 to 1200 degrees Celsius). Standard capacity is 10MT per hour. The furnace also incorporates hot charging technology.

View More

Roughing Mill and Intermediate Mill

The roughing mill includes Y-Table, proper guide box, twist pipe and auto feed repeaters. 1000 HP induction motor runs the roughing mill and intermediate mill, adjoined by a dynamically balanced 8 MT fly wheel.

View More

Finishing Mill and Auto-cooling Bed

10-inch Finishing Mill has a Continuous Bearing Mill of 2 stands driven by 400HP Slipring Induction Motor, capable of producing about 10 MT deformed bars per hour. The auto cooling bed handles hot bars in high speed operation and allows bars to cool in ambient temperature.

View More

Cold Shearing and Dispatch

High performance Cold Shearing Machine cuts bars to desired length. 50MT computerized Weigh Bridge ensures accuracy in weight. Delivery is made within 24 hours after order confirmation. Every delivery is accompanied by a dispatch card.
