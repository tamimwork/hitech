Premium Quality Scrap

Quality of scrap determines the quality of steel. Hi-Tech uses HMS1 (Heavy Melting Scrap 1) the premium quality and universally accredited scrap ensuring superior weldability and proper bendability per BDS standard.

02

Precision Production

World-class machineries for factory and lab to control quality in every phase of production. Spectrometric, chemical, and mechanical testing units ensure every bar meets specification.

Universally Certified

04
Manufacturing

From molten metal to monumental strength

Raw Material Sourcing

HMS1 (Heavy Melting Scrap 1), the premium quality and universally accredited scrap, is sourced and transported via Fork Lifter to the Induction Furnace for melting.

View More

Induction Melting Furnace

Hi-Tech has an Induction Melting Furnace with a capacity of 8 Metric ton. This high capacity unit is manufactured by Megatherm Furnaces Pvt. Ltd., India. Scraps are melted into liquid steel here.

View More

Re-heating Furnace

Natural gas fired re-heating furnace equipped with motor driven mechanical pusher. Steel Bars are heated in rolling temperature (1100 to 1200 degrees Celsius). Standard capacity is 10MT per hour.

View More

Roughing Mill and Intermediate Mill

The roughing mill includes Y-Table, proper guide box, twist pipe and auto feed repeaters. 1000 HP induction motor runs the roughing mill and intermediate mill, adjoined by a dynamically balanced 8 MT fly wheel.

View More

Finishing Mill and Auto-cooling Bed

10-inch Finishing Mill has a Continuous Bearing Mill of 2 stands driven by 400HP Slipring Induction Motor, capable of producing about 10 MT deformed bars per hour. The auto cooling bed handles hot bars in high speed operation and allows bars to cool in ambient temperature.

View More

Cold Shearing and Dispatch

High performance Cold Shearing Machine cuts bars to desired length. 50MT computerized Weigh Bridge ensures accuracy in weight. Delivery is made within 24 hours after order confirmation.
